
# ♻️ Dhrti – From Waste to Purpose

Dhrti is a full-stack B2B Sustainable Waste Exchange Platform that connects waste suppliers, recyclers, and industrial buyers through a digital marketplace.

The platform digitizes waste trading by enabling suppliers to list recyclable materials and buyers to discover, evaluate, and purchase waste resources efficiently. Dhrti promotes a circular economy by improving waste utilization, reducing landfill dependency, and supporting sustainable procurement practices.

## 🌍 Problem Statement

India generates millions of tons of recyclable waste every year. However, waste suppliers, aggregators, and recyclers often operate in fragmented ecosystems with limited digital connectivity.

This results in:

* Inefficient waste sourcing
* Limited market visibility
* Reduced recycling efficiency
* Higher procurement costs
* Underutilization of recyclable materials

Dhrti solves these challenges through a centralized digital marketplace that connects suppliers and buyers directly.
